## Thyroid–Skin Connection (Monitoring, not an active lever)

The April 2026 panel showed Total T4 (0.7) and Free T4 (<0.3, Zoetis) both low, prompting a May 2026 MSU Premium Thyroid Panel (gold standard). Result: only TT4 out of range; Free T4 by equilibrium dialysis at floor of normal (6 pmol/L, ref 6–42); TSH normal (0.28); all autoantibodies negative. **Decision: do NOT treat.** Low-normal thyroid values can still contribute to suboptimal skin barrier integrity, sebaceous gland function, and local immune defenses (and may worsen Malassezia), but thyroid optimization is **no longer an actionable treatment lever** — it becomes a monitoring item on annual panels. If TSH rises or Free T4 drops below the MSU floor on a future panel, the treatment question reopens. (Full thyroid detail in buddy-health-history.md.)
