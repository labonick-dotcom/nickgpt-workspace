## Sales Career Timeline

- **Gartner (~2015-2017, ~2 years):** First real sales role; learned research-based selling. Ended due to "poor judgment and boundary issues." Described as formative and humbling. (Documented honestly for the first time in the 2026 therapy intake.)
- **Qualtrics (~2017, ~4 months):** Brief stint; ended after "inappropriate comments." A painful lesson in managing impulses professionally; reflects immaturity and lack of emotional regulation at the time.
- **Salesforce (~2017-2019, ~2 years):** Where he stabilized professionally; learned enterprise SaaS sales at scale; built confidence.

These early exits (Gartner, Qualtrics) are framed as pivotal lessons that led him to take professional development seriously.
