## Use Cases (Prioritized)

**Tier 1 -- Do these first. Frees up capacity, builds trust.**

1. **Client Delivery Workspace** -- Pre-built workflow templates for each engagement type (discovery call, immersion day, retreat, strategy session, follow-up). Everything Kara and Allison need is waiting when they open the workspace. This is the bottleneck fix Daniel called out on the call.
2. **Fathom → Claude Automation** -- Direct connector or workflow. Transcript comes out of Fathom, flows into the right client project, triggers meeting notes, action items, follow-up draft. One click, done.
3. **Kara Bot (Ask Kara)** -- White-label custom GPT trained on all Kara's content, voice, methodologies. Same model as Ask Ian. Gives clients 24/7 access to her frameworks. Can be tiered into subscription model. Biggest scale lever.
4. **OneNote Integration / Migration** -- Either connect OneNote to Claude or migrate that operational data somewhere that connects. This is the single biggest data gap in their stack.

**Tier 2 -- High value, slightly more lift.**

5. **Content Generation Engine** -- Kara talks, you get 2 months of content. Transcripts → LinkedIn posts, newsletters, podcast outlines, lead magnets. Brian is already doing a version of this manually, just slow. Automate and scale it.
6. **Systems Audit + Consolidation** -- Map SharePoint, OneNote, Monday.com, HubSpot, Kajabi. Kill redundancies. Propose a leaner stack that talks to itself and to Claude natively.
7. **Digital Products from Existing Content** -- 10 years of content. Courses, curricula, subscription resources. Turn past engagement material into scalable offerings. Huge margin play.

**Tier 3 -- Phase 2, after capacity is freed.**

8. **AI Resident Expert for Allegro's CEO Clients** -- Nick or Daniel as AI-on-staff, same model as UYSP. Brian lit up on this. Allegro's clients are business owners trying to put AI into their organizations -- that's a different and bigger audience than Ian's sellers. Workshops, keynotes, enablement.
9. **Lead Gen Automation** -- Grade/score new leads, follow-up workflows, intake forms → proposals. Brian isn't ready for this yet (no capacity for more clients), but it's the path to the $10M goal.
10. **Subscription Model Infrastructure** -- CRM cleanup (HubSpot or headless CRM), subscription billing, automated onboarding flow for the mastermind they're rebuilding with Brooke.
