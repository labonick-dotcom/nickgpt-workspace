## Website Requirements (Detailed)

### Design Principles
- Production-professional, impressive — "if our website doesn't look like the ones we're pitching, we lose credibility"
- Modern UI, 3D animations, movement — top-tier visual quality
- No filler words, no bloat. Simple language, digestible on first read
- Gary Vee / Tony Robbins / Alex Hormozi-level storytelling and branding
- Emotion target: "Oh my God, this will save my business. I can't miss this."
- AEO / AIE / SEO optimized

### Required Sections
1. **Hero** — clear value prop, emotional hook, CTA
2. **Services / Use Cases** — each workflow broken out by business outcome, not feature
3. **Process** — discovery → intake → data digestion → delivery (simple, visual)
4. **About** — Nick (MBA LSU data analytics, Gartner, Qualtrics, Salesforce, Microsoft, Fortune 500 clients) + Daniel (major professional services firm — DO NOT SAY RSM, anonymize; Microsoft Gold partner framing is fine)
5. **Case Studies / Examples** — Christi story, screenshots from real projects, dashboards
6. **Client Logos** — IgKnight, Untap Your Sales Potential, Brad's company (Pluralsight area)
7. **Testimonials** — Daniel has 13 reviews from AI for the Strategic Seller course (includes Google, Salesforce users); stored in Daniel's OneDrive, to be shared
8. **Lead Capture** — automated (emails Nick + Daniel); framed as pre-assessment / ROI calculator intake
9. **Footer / Social proof**

### Lead Magnet / Intake Concept
- Pre-assessment: "How much time are you losing to manual work?" → calculator → business value estimate
- Gets them to submit form; auto-routes to Nick + Daniel
