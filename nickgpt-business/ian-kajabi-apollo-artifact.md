## What We're Building

A pre-call intelligence system for your 4 BDRs. Every prospect that enters the pipeline gets automatically enriched before the BDR picks up the phone.

**Input:** LinkedIn profile + any available prospect info
**Output:** A pre-call brief with:
- Personality and communication style analysis
- How qualified they are (budget appetite, tier prediction)
- Coach match recommendation tailored to this prospect
- Objection library built from real UYSP Fathom transcripts
- Personalized follow-up copy ready to send post-call

No more generic outreach. No more cold calls where the BDR is figuring out the person while they're on the phone with them.
