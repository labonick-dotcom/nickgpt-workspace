# Client: Christi Civalier

> Summary: Christi Civalier is Nick's first paying external client — a 37-year sales veteran and UYSP coach. Nick built her a full Claude Cowork operating workspace (Phase 1 delivered May 29, 2026, now in active use with 15+ tool-generated emails). The engagement forged Nick's coaching-ops build playbook and his data-integrity methodology. Phase 2 and a second personal-business workspace build are confirmed next.

## Who Christi Is

- **37-year sales veteran**, recently retired from corporate.
- **UYSP coach** — coaches enterprise tech sellers in Ian Koniak's Untap Your Sales Potential program. The **only UYSP coach who came up through the program as a member.**
- Warm, high-energy, ideas-first, **non-technical.**
- Has ~80 clients (grew from 46 at discovery), coaching monthly/bi-monthly.

## Origin / How They Met

Christi is a UYSP coach; the engagement is Nick's first external implementation client and the worked reference case for the coaching-ops Cowork build playbook.

## The Pain (in her own words)

After each call she manually copied a transcript into ChatGPT, generated a summary, pasted it into a shared Google Doc, and sent the link. Chronically behind, clients waiting. **Emotional core: never process a transcript at 9pm after her son's lacrosse game again.**

## Engagement History

**Phase 1 DELIVERED (May 29, 2026):** full Claude Cowork workspace on her computer:
- ~80 client folders (each: client-profile.md, coach-intel.md, session-notes.md, intake-notes.md, transcripts/)
- A voice-capture folder, a methodology folder, a CLAUDE.md operating contract
- Context layer (voice-and-tone, program methodology, coaching-philosophy, voice-library)
- Four working skills: **new-client-intake, pre-call-prep, post-call-summary, progress-scorecard**
- **Fathom + Gmail connectors**, companion Claude.ai project, WhisperFlow voice dictation
- All four skills demoed live on the call and worked.

**Enrichment & correction work (June 1, 2026):** processed 30 RHETI Enneagram PDFs + 31 intake docs, created 9 new client folders, enriched ~26 existing profiles, wrote 9 coaching playbooks. A content-fidelity audit found **four data-integrity errors** (a transposed code, a wrong email, a scraped vendor phone number, an unsupported payment plan) plus several collapsed Enneagram ties — all corrected in a verified pass. This engagement **forged and proved Nick's data-integrity methodology.**

**Hosting model (important):** the workspace lives in **Dropbox, not Google Drive** (Cowork's folder sync only works with Dropbox online; Google Drive has a VM-mounting bug with Cowork on Mac, iCloud has data-loss risk). Nick hosts it so both he and Christi read/write the same folder and he can push updates without re-zipping. This is effectively a **managed-services model** and the template for hosting future client workspaces.

## Revenue / Structure

- **Phase 1 + Phase 2 at $1,500 total** (founding-client rate, deliberately below market to establish the reference and the relationship).
- **Phase 2 (charged separately):** a Google Form for new-client intake wired into the intake skill (form → Sheet → skill), plus a second full workspace build for Christi's new personal coaching business.
- **Personal-business build confirmed as the next paid engagement.**

## Relationship Quality / Dynamics

- Strong. Christi installed her own skill zips despite being non-technical (with guidance).
- **Christi's description of Nick:** contrasted him with her MIT/Harvard-trained business coach — she is the refined academic, **Nick is "School of Hard Knocks."**
- Christi said she does **not have the patience to learn the tooling**; she just wants to know what Nick thinks and how much. (Core differentiator: Nick builds it for her, doesn't teach her.)
- Christi is a referral engine: offering intros to Allison LeCousier, Carolina, the SheSells community under Elise Archer.

## What Worked / What Didn't

- Worked: voice-in/output-out design, Cowork-only (no Make/Zapier/code), Dropbox sync, propose-then-execute discipline.
- Lesson learned the hard way: verifying "the write landed" is not enough — must compare back to source. This is the **defining lesson of the engagement.**

## Status & Next Steps

- Phase 1 live and in active use (15+ tool-generated follow-up emails sent). Nick running a mass enrichment/update on her workspace.
- **Known blocker for the personal-business build:** Claude connects to one Google account at a time, and her two businesses use different Google accounts — sequence the connector context before building.
- Strategically: Christi's workspace is **the template.** Architecture replicates for other coaches at ~40-60% less build time. The UYSP program founder approved deployment to the full coaching team; a second coach, **Mike Fiascone, already requested his own** (next scaling target). Also the proof case for a one-to-many teaching model.
