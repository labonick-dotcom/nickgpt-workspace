### What the Retainer Covers (Monthly Recurring Deliverables)

1. **Monthly 90-minute AI Office Hours** — live Q&A + curriculum for Gold and Platinum members (scheduled through Kajabi, consistent with existing office-hours cadence).
2. **Ask Ian maintenance** — monthly content refresh and minor prompt adjustments to the Ask Ian GPT.
3. **Resident AI positioning** across UYSP materials (member comms, onboarding, program materials, marketing).
4. **Periodic participation in UYSP's Wednesday office hours**, cadence coordinated with Ian's team.
5. **Strategic advisory** — a standing 1-2 hour monthly call with Isaac, Tanveer, or other internal team members (the advisory agreement says **up to 4 hours/month**), including building lightweight internal tools. Cadence established via a 30-60 min kickoff call with Isaac and Tanveer.
6. **Weekly Business Scorecard** (per the advisory agreement) — a Google Sheets dashboard updated weekly. Metrics: new meetings held (new prospects only), closed deals, conversion % (new meetings vs closed deals), average selling price and product mix. Source data: Kajabi exports and master roster, coordinated with Isaac.

The retainer includes one short-term project each month. Examples of included builds: **Territory Planner GPT**, **UYSP Light buildout**, **Ask Ian coach-voice expansion**.
