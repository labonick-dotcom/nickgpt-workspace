# UYSP: Projects & Tools

> Summary: Every UYSP project and tool — delivered, in flight, future, and killed. Covers Territory Planner GPT, UYSP Light, Ask Ian (GPT, coach-voice expansion, and the Reasoning Layer), the Weekly Business Scorecard, and the future project queue (Renewal Intelligence Workflow, Internal Ops GPT, BDR Performance System, Monday Morning Scorecard/Narrative). Everything ties to Ian's two levers: conversions and renewals.

## Project Scope Rule

One short-term project per month is included in the retainer. Projects may be swapped monthly if something more strategic arises, subject to Nick's approval. Everything must connect to **(1) new meetings and conversions** or **(2) renewals.**

## Ask Ian (the GPT)

**Ask Ian** is the production custom GPT scaling access to Ian's methodology. Positioning: **"Ask Ian is scalable access to Ian's methodology, not a chatbot."** Never call it "a chatbot" or "an AI assistant." Member-facing name is just "Ask Ian" (not "Ask Ian GPT"). Monthly maintenance (content refresh + minor prompt adjustments) is a recurring retainer deliverable.

**Ask Ian GPT 2.0** is a future downstream consumer of the private Master Reservoir (see methodologies / Ask Ian Reasoning Layer).

## Ask Ian Reasoning Layer (Built for Coaching Clients)

A reusable system baking Ian's strategic reasoning into a coaching client's custom Claude project — three-artifact architecture (Master Reservoir [private], Ask Ian Knowledge File [Core ~2,500 words + optional Appendix ~1,800 words], Project Instruction Block). Built and validated June 2026; first deployment Brad Figler, validated on a live T-Mobile scenario. Full methodology in methodologies.md. (IP-relationship angle: surface to Ian at the July quarterly review.)

## Weekly Business Scorecard

Per the advisory agreement: a **Google Sheets dashboard updated weekly.** Metrics: new meetings held (new prospects only), closed deals, conversion % (new meetings vs closed deals), average selling price and product mix. Source data: Kajabi exports + master roster, coordinated with Isaac.
